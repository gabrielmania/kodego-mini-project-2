About Us

History

Mr. Ginbert Aterado is an avid cyclist. His hobby stems way back from his childhood. His love for cycling encourages him to participate in events and competitions localy and internationaly. As a hobbyist and a professional cyclists, he found that there are very limited bike shops that can cater various services to it's customer. Whether they may be a beginner, a hobbyists, or a professional. With that in mind, Mr. Aterado decided to open a small bike shop funded by his winnings from the competitions he participated in. Thus, Ginyard Bike Shop & Repair was born on June 1995. The shop was the first of it's kind here in the Philippines. Not only Mr. Aterado offers bikes, parts, and essential gears. He also offers bike services that are hard to find locally. With the success of his first store, Mr. Aterado decides to open various branches to cater clients from different parts of the country.

Mission

Our mission is to provide quality biking products that caters every type of cyclists and to provide them quality services to take care of their bikes. Whethey they just need a simple repair, to a bike wash, to bike coating for additional protection, and other services. We have it here in Ginyard Bike Shop & Repair.

Vision

Our vision is to provide accessible biking products and services to every Filipino whether you are just a starting cyclist or a professional. We want to be a "onestop shop" for all your biking needs!
